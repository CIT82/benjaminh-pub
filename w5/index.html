Fear the Flemoids!

Here's a quick rundown on the creeps you might meet in Chex Quest

Flemoidus Commonus

Common Man
Slow, weak, and none-too-bright, this feller wanders around a bit like a zombie, man.

Flemoidus Bipedicus

Slighty Less Common Man
A bit faster thanks to his two legs, and blows his nose in a shotgun-like pattern.

Armored Flemoidus Bipedicus

Metal Man
Slower, sturdier, and fires a slow-moving slime projectile. Also has a melee attack. Overall impish nature.

Flemoidus Cycloptis

One-Eyed Fellow
Lacks a ranged attack, but beware its speed and melee attack! On higher difficulties acts like a sort speed demon.

The Flembrane

Thick Fella
Big, slow, and plenty of health. Beware its projectile attack and deadly melee attack.
